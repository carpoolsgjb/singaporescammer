Ministers Deny Links to Su Haijin Amid Photo Controversy

On 6 May 2025, Health Minister Ong Ye Kung and Transport Minister Chee Hong Tat denied links to convicted money launderer Su Haijin after photos emerged showing them at social events Su attended.

They stated they were guests of a friend and did not know Su personally. However, the photos also include prominent names like Lim Swee Say, Sam Goi, and Oei Hong Leong—raising concerns about elite access and influence.

Background on Su Haijin’s Crimes

Su, a Cypriot national, was arrested on 15 August 2023 and convicted in April 2024 for:

  • Money laundering
  • Using forged documents
  • False declarations
  • Evading arrest

He was deported after serving part of a 14-month sentence. Authorities seized over S$170 million from Su. The broader probe led to more than S$3 billion in frozen assets linked to the Fujian gang.
